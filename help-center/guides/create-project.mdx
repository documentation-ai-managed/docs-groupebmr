## Overview

Projects organize your documentation. Follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    Click "Projects" in the left sidebar after logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click the "New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter project details">
    Give your project a name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create" to finish setup. Your project appears in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
