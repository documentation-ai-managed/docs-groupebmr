## Overview

To access your Groupebmr documentation account:

1. Visit `https://docs.groupebmr.com/login`
2. Enter your registered email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## What if I forgot my password?

- Click "Forgot Password?"
- Check your email for reset instructions
- Set a new password and log in

Need more help? Email [support@groupebmr.com](mailto:support@groupebmr.com).
