## Create Your Account

Sign up for Groupebmr in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://app.groupebmr.com/signup` and click **Get Started for Free**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
    
    <Callout kind="tip">
      Use a strong password with at least 12 characters.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Groupebmr and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page and sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Organize your project docs in dedicated spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space** in the top right.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs`, select **Public** or **Private** visibility, and click **Create**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Welcome Page" icon="book-open">
    Your space opens with a default homepage. Rename it by clicking the title.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Add and Edit Your First Page

Start documenting with Markdown or MDX.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown" icon="edit-3">
    Click **New Page** > **Markdown**.
    
````markdown
# My First Page

Welcome to Groupebmr!

## Features
- Easy editing
- Live previews
````
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="MDX" icon="code">
    Click **New Page** > **MDX** for components.
    
````mdx
---
title: Advanced Page
---

## Hello World

<Callout kind="success">MDX powers interactive docs!</Callout>
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Publish by clicking **Save** then **Publish**.
